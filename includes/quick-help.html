The timeline is vertical

  • The dot indicates where in the song the player is currently at.
  • The purple part of the timeline indicates what part of the song that will be played.
  • You can put markers next to the timeline.

0:42
  • Clicking the marker name () makes the song start at that time (42 seconds)
  • The -button makes the song stop at that time
